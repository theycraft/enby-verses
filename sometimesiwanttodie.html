Sometimes I Want to Die

Published on 5th January 2025

Sometimes I want to sink and drown,
to let the water pull me down.
Sometimes I want to leap and fly,
to touch the ground and close my sky.

Sometimes I want to choke the air,
to end the weight I cannot bear.
Sometimes I want to bleed and fade,
to let the red erase the blade.

Sometimes I want to meet a train,
to leave the world and end the pain,
Sometimes I want to swallow sleep,
to drift where none can reach too deep

Sometimes I want to slip, let go,
to disappear before the glow.
Sometimes I want to die, but find,
it's not my life, it's just my mind.

Jayden C
